Instructions on how to make the rice:

Making Sprouted California Rice

Enter how many ounces of rice you want to make

To make the rice slightly al dente:

The ratio (everything in ounces) of rice to water or broth to olive oil is 1: 1.6: 0.05

The other ingredients you will need:

ounces of water or broth

ounces of olive oil

Instructions on how to make the rice al dente:

Combine the ounces of rice with ounces of water or broth and ounces of olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

To make the rice softer:

The ratio (everything in ounces) of rice to water or broth to olive oil is 1: 2: 0.05

The other ingredients you will need:

ounces of water or broth

ounces of olive oil

Instructions on how to make the rice softer:

Combine the ounces of rice with ounces of water or broth and ounces of olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 30 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.
